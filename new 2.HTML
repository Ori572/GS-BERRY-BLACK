About Us

The whole point of the band is to establish new talents, skills and tactics from the kasi to the world. We are aiming to grow the brand with as we grab each and every person's attention. In conclusion, the next coming year are reserved for this brand to be distributed in our African Stores and abroad for numerous job creations and youth skills development
